About me

Hello! My name is John Mary, and I am an aspiring developer from Kenya with a passion for creating websites. My journey into web development began during a high school STEM contest, which ignited my fascination with coding and technology. Since then, I have been dedicated to honing my skills and expanding my knowledge in the field.

Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Statistics and Programming at Mama Ngina University College. My academic journey has provided me with a strong foundation in both statistical analysis and software development, allowing me to merge these disciplines to solve complex problems and create innovative solutions.

In addition to my studies, I have a deep interest in data analytics. I have developed a robust understanding and practical experience in both qualitative and quantitative analysis. This involves not only the theoretical aspects but also the practical application of various data analysis tools and technologies. My expertise includes working with tools such as Python, R, MySQL, Excel, Microsoft PowerBI, SPSS, Stata, ODK, Nvivo and Tableau, which I use to extract insights from data and support data-driven decision-making.

Outside of academics and professional development, I enjoy creating websites as a hobby. This passion project has allowed me to experiment with different web technologies, frameworks, and design principles, further enhancing my coding skills and creativity. My goal is to continuously improve and contribute to the tech community through innovative web solutions and insightful data analyses.

I am always eager to learn and take on new challenges, whether it's mastering a new programming language, exploring advanced statistical methods, or developing a unique web application. I believe that the intersection of web development and data analytics holds immense potential, and I am excited to be at the forefront of this dynamic field.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I look forward to connecting with like-minded individuals and exploring opportunities for collaboration and growth.
